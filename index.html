The Educational and Psychological Alliance for Wellness, Inc. exists to provide educational, psychological, and mental health support to individuals and families who may otherwise be unable to access these services. EPAW focuses on underserved communities, including families with limited financial resources, students with learning or behavioral concerns, and adults seeking clarity, support, and advocacy. Our goal is simple: help people receive the right information, support, and next steps before a challenge becomes a crisis. PROGRAMS Fundable programs with measurable impact. EPAW organizes its work into clear program pathways so donors, sponsors, and grantmakers can see exactly how support becomes impact. 🧠 Evaluation Access Fund Helps children, teens, and adults access psychological, psychoeducational, autism, ADHD, learning, and social-emotional evaluations when cost is the primary barrier. Sponsor an Evaluation 💬 Mental Health & Wellness Support Provides counseling support, wellness education, referral guidance, and intervention resources for individuals and families navigating emotional or behavioral concerns. Fund Wellness Support 🎓 Parent & Student Advocacy Academy Equips families with practical tools to understand school supports, IEPs, 504 plans, evaluations, accommodations, and next steps after receiving results. Support Training 🤝 School & Community Partnership Program Partners with schools, churches, civic groups, pediatric offices, and community organizations to deliver education, screenings, workshops, and referral pathways.
