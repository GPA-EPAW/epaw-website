About EPAW

Built to close the access gap.

The Educational and Psychological Alliance for Wellness, Inc. exists to provide educational, psychological, and mental health support to individuals and families who may otherwise be unable to access these services.

EPAW focuses on underserved communities, including families with limited financial resources, students with learning or behavioral concerns, and adults seeking clarity, support, and advocacy.

Our goal is simple: help people receive the right information, support, and next steps before a challenge becomes a crisis.
